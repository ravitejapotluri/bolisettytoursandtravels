About Us

Bolisetty Travel & Tours is a family-owned tour operator and travel agency located in Vijayawada, India. We specialize in tours to Domestic(India) but are always expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new and exciting tours to International . A family-owned business based in Vijayawada, Bolisetty Tours And Travel was founded in 2000 by Bolisetty Rama Krishna and since that humble beginning it has grown into a trusted resource that has helped tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.
